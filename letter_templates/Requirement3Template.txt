The petition failed to note the reason that homeowners are petitioning for mediation. Pursuant to Florida Statutes 723.037(5)(a)(1) through 723.037(5)(a)(4), homeowners may petition for one or more of the following reasons:

1. The lot rental increase is unreasonable.
2. The lot rental increase has made the total lot rental amount unreasonable.
3. The decrease in services or utilities is not accompanied by a corresponding decrease or is otherwise unreasonable. 
4. The change in rules and regulations is unreasonable.

Please indicate one or more of these reasons for petitioning for mediation in order to correct this deficiency.
